Certified Financial Fiduciary, Insurance Producer and Retirement Planner

Archer Life Financial LLC mission is to serve our clients in all areas of their financial needs including asset protection, tax planning and investments. We call this “Complete Benefits of Ownership” or C.B.O. Our primary focus is helping clients to protect assets, qualify for state & federal benefits and preserve investment funds with guaranteed* growth and income.

Jeff Archer, Certified Financial Fiduciary has been helping his clients (Business Owners, Executives and Families) since 2001 with their retirement and protection planning. Jeff specializes in strategies that teach people how to maximize all their assets and build a retirement in a safe and tax-efficient manner. With all the economic turmoil in the markets, the country and around the world, it’s more important than ever to keep your wealth safe and secure ... but growing.

Jeff and his wife Lisa have been married 27 years and have a 20 years old son in college. Jeff Archer, CFF graduated from Bowling Green State University. Jeff is surrounded by friends around the country from his Singing Barbershop experiences since the mid 80’s and eventually being honored with the silver Medal (2nd place in the World) in 2001 at the International Harmony Society Quartet Competition in Nashville. Now a Donor and former member of the Barbershop Harmony Society.

Jeff’s mission is to help clients avoid losing money in the Wall Street Casino, protecting principal assets and develop final expense, legacy, estate, and retirement distribution plans.
