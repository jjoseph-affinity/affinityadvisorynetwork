Doug Sanders

Insurance Producer and Retirement Planner

Douglas A. Sanders takes great pride in his work as a post-retirement professional. Drawing on his years coaching college athletes, he understands the power of a team. He brought that same team approach to his work with TransAmerica. He earned his Life license from the State of Ohio and used it to begin providing his clients with the very best insurance options. This work inspired him to seek more team members to help provide his clients with more complete planning, better protections, income, and distributions.

So, he immersed himself in learning the intricate details of retirement and Estate protection planning, Social Security and Medicare, Long-Term Care options and solutions, and Veterans benefits.

Doug began his career at Affinity shadowing the founder and president extensively. During this close-up work, Doug experienced the specialized Estate protection strategies specifically created to achieve the goals of the retired senior. Doug has contributed to the development of highly customized plans and has helped to protect the lifestyle and legacy of Affinity clients.

Because the multi-disciplinary network is the foundation of all planning, the proper coordination of each professional is critical. Doug will make sure each professional makes their contribution exactly as necessary to help you achieve your goals.

Doug has focused his practice on families in Northeast Ohio. He often consults with the CEO of Affinity on customized plans for his clients. So, if your goals are to protect your money and property from Probate and long-term care facilities, create endless income, minimize taxes, or to maximize distributions to your spouse or children, Doug will be the coach that will get you there!
